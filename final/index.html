Company Information

DanDan's Ramyun House opened in December 10, 2005 by the founder SiChan Park. Our shop sells varieties of tradtional Korean ramyun including Shin Ramyun and Kimchi Ramyun. Our shop is located in many countries in the world. The countries include Thailand, Korea, United States of America, and England. It's always good for you.
